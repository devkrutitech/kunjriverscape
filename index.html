Welcome to a life fulfilled, a life full of nature's goodness, of fresh air, flowing waters, pristine & green surroundings.

Kunj Riverscape is a delightful campus developed on the banks of the River Dhadhar, off Dabhoi Road- a quick 30 minutes drive from Vadodara. A project which is our labour of love, offers Retreat Homes set facing the river. Providing an ideal setting to relax and to enjoy the goodness of nature

The Retreat Vilas are desianed such that thev offer a liberal view of the river and the greens surrounding the campus, and the campus itself. The 2-BHK living space is perfect for you family and can easily accommodate your guests who are lucky to have a host like you.
